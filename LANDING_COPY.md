## FAQ

**Is this actually free?**
Yes. The audit is free, the results are free, the share URL is free. We capture your email if you want the report sent to you — that's the only ask.

**How accurate are the savings numbers?**
Every number comes from real pricing data, not AI estimates. We check official pricing pages and update the data regularly. The rules are deterministic — same inputs always produce the same output.

**What tools do you support?**
Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Anthropic API, OpenAI API, Gemini, and Windsurf. More coming soon.

**Do you store my spend data?**
Your spend data stays in your browser (localStorage). We only store audit results in our database when you use the share URL feature — and even then, spend amounts are stripped from the public version.

**What if I'm already on the right plans?**
We'll tell you. About 30% of audits come back fully optimized. That's a useful answer too — it means you can stop second-guessing your AI budget.

**What's Credex Pro?**
Coming soon: ongoing spend monitoring, Slack alerts when pricing changes, and team dashboards. Join the waitlist by submitting your email after your audit.
